History of Benteng Kuto Besak

Benteng Kuto Besak, meaning "Square Fortress," was built in the 18th century during the Palembang Sultanate era. Constructed to defend against Dutch colonial invasions, this fortress showcases a unique blend of Malay and European architectural influences. Its thick walls, bastions, and central courtyard stand as a testament to Palembang's resistance and cultural heritage.
